Code of Conduct

Midwest Big Data Summer School is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, age or religion. We do not tolerate harassment of summer school participants in any form. Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate for any summer school venue, including talks. Summer school participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the summer school at the discretion of the school organizers.

Midwest Big Data Summer School participants are also bound by Iowa State University policies on discrimination and harassment.

If you hear an inappropriate remark, intended or misjudged, this list of comebacks may prove useful.

These policies have been adopted from Geek Feminism Wiki, which includes models for public announcements and guidance for event staff.
