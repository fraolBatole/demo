<P>

<P>

<B><U>Application Status:</U></B><BR>
Please locate your CS ID tracking number in the table below.  
<P>
<B class=red>Incomplete</B> -- Materials are missing and application will not be 
evaluated.  It may take 4-6 weeks for your official TOEFL
and GRE scores to arrive at ISU after test completion so please allow for this delay.  
<P>

<P>
Official GRE scores are required for full admittance.  
<P>
<B class=red>In Evaluation</B> -- Application is complete and sent to the 
review committee.  Spring application review will not start until 
after September 1. 
<P>
<B class=red>Denied</B> -- Application has been denied.  Due to the large number 
of applications received, we are unable to give individual reasons for 
denial.  
Some reasons for denial may include low GRE scores, low GPA, weak letters 
of recommendation, or incomplete background in Computer 
Science.  
<P>
<B class=red>Deactivated</B> -- Application is incomplete and student has not 
responded to requests to provide missing materials.  Admissions 
evaluation has been returned to Graduate Admissions office.  
<P>
<B class=red>Hold</B> -- Application has passed a 
first review and is awaiting an additional review. 
<P>
<B class=red>Admit</B> -- Student has been admitted to our graduate program. 
<P>
<B class=red>Funding</B> -- TA position availability for spring will not be known until October 
or November.  </a><P>

Please keep checking your online status for further information.  
<P>
Thank you for your patience.<P>
