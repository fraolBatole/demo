Music and Social Gatherings

During Summer: Ames on the Half Shell - Friday evening concerts in the Bandshell Park (Intersection of Duff Ave and 6th Street).

Sports

During Football and Basketball Season: Check out the schedule for Iowa State Cyclones.

Theater

  • Stephens Auditorium at Iowa State Events Center

Other Sources of Ongoing Events

  • http://www.ames365.com/: a community website for Ames locals, includes an events calendar (sometimes slow, try searching on google and looking at the cached version of the website).
  • http://event.iastate.edu/: Iowa State University's official events calendar
  • http://www.ames.lib.ia.us/: Ames Public Library's events calendar

Finally, if you can't find anything, there is Cinema

  • Cinema @ North Grand Mall: dollar theater in Ames, well it's not quite a dollar, but still super cheap to watch a movie, if you are willing to wait for a month or so to watch it.
  • Movie 12: another slightly more expensive theater on South Duff Avenue.
  • Movies in Campustown: usually plays great movies.
