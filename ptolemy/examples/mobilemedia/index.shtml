MobileMedia Example

We present a detailed empirical study to evaluate the effectiveness of PtolemyJ's design by applying it to a series of architectural releases of a software product line (SPL) for handling multimedia on mobile devices, called MobileMedia and the comparison and contrast of our findings with a previous in-depth analysis of the object-oriented and aspect-oriented design of the same system by Figueiredo et al.
