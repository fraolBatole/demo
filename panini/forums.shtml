Panini Users

Current or potential Panini users may seek clarifications on the language and its infrastructure by posting to paninij-users mailing list.

To post to this discussion list, send an email to:

ppaninij-users@lists.sourceforge.net

General information about this mailing list is available at:

https://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/paninij-users

The link above also provides a web-based mechanism to subscribe to this mailing list.

The archived discussion about Panini is available at:

http://sourceforge.net/mailarchive/forum.php?forum_name=paninij-users

Panini Developers

To seek clarifications on the language implementation, to contribute to the development of Panini's infrastructure, or to simply follow the development of the Panini programming language going forward please post to paninij-commit mailing list. Notifications for any changes in the repositories for the Panni project are also sent to this mailing list.

To post to this discussion list, send an email to:

paninij-commit@lists.sourceforge.net

General information about this mailing list is available at:

https://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/paninij-commit

The link above also provides a web-based mechanism to subscribe to this mailing list.

An archive containing messages for this mailing list is available at:

http://sourceforge.net/mailarchive/forum.php?forum_name=paninij-commit
