Iowa State University

These pages describe work carried out on the design and the implementation of the Panini programming language. The PI is Hridesh Rajan from Iowa State University. This work is supported in part by the US National Science Foundation (NSF) under grants CCF-14-23370, CCF-08-46059, CCF-10-17334, and CCF-11-17937 to Rajan. Any opinions, findings, and conclusions or recommendations expressed in this material are those of the author(s) and do not necessarily reflect the views of the NSF.
